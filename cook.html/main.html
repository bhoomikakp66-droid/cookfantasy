Our core dishes


BREAKFAST

In this you will get authentic south Indian breakfast recipes.
South Indian breakfast is known for its rich flavors, vibrant colors, and wholesome ingredients.
Breakfast are typically light yet filling, providing energy for the day ahead, and are enjoyed with accompaniments that add layers of taste and texture.
Served fresh and warm, this breakfast tradition reflects the region's deep-rooted culture of hospitality and nourishment.


LUNCH

In this you will get authentic lunch recipes.
South Indian lunch is a vibrant and satisfying affair, built around freshly steamed rice served with a variety of flavorful accompaniments. The meal usually features a mix of spiced vegetable curries, tangy and aromatic gravies, lentil-based dishes, and crispy fried sides, all enhanced with the fragrance of curry leaves, mustard seeds, and coconut.

SNACKS

In this you will get snacks recipes.
Snacks are light, flavorful bites enjoyed between meals or as a quick treat.
They can range from crispy and crunchy to soft and
savory, often made with simple ingredients but bursting with taste.
Perfect for satisfying small hunger pangs, snacks are a great way to keep energy levels up
and add a bit of indulgence to the day. Whether paired with a hot drink or enjoyed on their own, they bring comfort and enjoyment in every bite.
